PROTECTING OWNER IP

//How our system works to keep your idea yours

  1. Protecting IP and not being a jerk are part of our User Agreement.
  2. We don't allow lurking. When users sign up, they have 2 days to post their own ideas, or they lose access to the community.
  3. Ideas go through our selection process that judges for audacity, impact, and uniqueness (absolutely no repeats. We will connect people with similar interests though, so you can collaborate, and maybe even find a co-founder!).

BENEFITS OF JOINING

//What you get out of joining our community

  1. Feedback is like gold. And its private. Even if someone tried to take your idea, they wouldn't get the "wisdom of the crowd" that you have access to.
  2. Life gets busy. And sometimes, it gets in the way of our real dreams. We push you forward towards launch with our method, motivational reminders, peer motivation, feedback, and expert advice.
